Problem

UM can analyze and measure the effectiveness of mobile ads at driving in-store visits. The problem was difficulty in requesting, managing and visualization reports. Sharing process for reports with clients was a manual process and not productizable. The primary goal was to find a way to make requesting reports easier. The secondary goal design a automatic visualization of reports. This small ask would turn into one of UberMedia's core business offerings.

My Role

Worked as the product manager in addition to the product designer and UI developer. We discovered pain-points, managed stakeholder expectations, wireframed as well as prototyped the flows/solutions, designed the visual brand, led the team, and developed the UI. Three hats are a lot of hats.
