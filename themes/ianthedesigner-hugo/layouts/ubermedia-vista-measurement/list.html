Background

UberMedia is a mobile data location company that help medium and large business make informed business decisions. UberMedia provides the highest quality mobile data solutions trusted by businesses to creatively solve their persistent challenges.

Problem

UberMedia can analyze and measure the effectiveness of mobile ads at driving in-store visits. Problems lay in requesting, managing and visualization reports. The process is too technical and tedious for clients to do on their own and isn't scalable at the companies current size.

The primary goal was to find a way to make requesting and visualization of reports a scalable product and process. Foreshadow alert: this small ask would turn into one of UberMedia's core business offerings.

My Role

Worked as the product manager in addition to the product designer and UI developer. We discovered pain-points, managed stakeholder expectations, wireframed as well as prototyped the flows/solutions, designed the visual brand, led the team, and developed the UI. Three hats are a lot of hats.
