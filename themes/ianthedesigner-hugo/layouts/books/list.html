52 books in 52 weeks

During COVID lockdown in Berlin, people didn't leave their flats. I couldn't stay cooped up especially with a dog. So I would listen to an audiobook and go on a two hour walk each night. In 2020 I listen to 43 audiobooks. So in 2021, my goal was a minimum one a week, or "52 books in 52 weeks."
