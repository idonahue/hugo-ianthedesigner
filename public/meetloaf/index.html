Meatloaf is a mobile consumer application for helping friends, colleagues, and daters to decide when and where to meet. Meatloaf let 2-10 people quickly swipe through a list of restaurants, bars, and coffee shops to find a perfect gathering place that everyone can be excited about. Meatloaf is tinder for group gatherings.

Problem

For some people and groups its easy to know where you are going. "It's Friday night, I'm going to be at Mac's Bar with the guys," or "I always meet colleagues at our normal coffee shop." For others, organizing, planning and communicating where to meet can be much bigger of a pain point.

Finding a date, time and location is full of friction points. Starting a group text is simple but getting everyone to agree isn't. The friction of organizing should not be a a reason to not meet.

Solution

Meetloaf is a mobile application developed by the team at Peerio Labs to help be a fast, easy and fun way to answer the when and where of meeting. Meetloaf works on iOS, Android and mobile web and can be used with out an account.

Choose a date and time, invite your friends and start swiping. Meetloaf will gather location data from all participants, and builds a short list of coffee shops, bars and restaurants. Everyone can start swiping and once their is a match, the location is messaged to all participants.
